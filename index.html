5 Basic Drills to do Before Running

For many people running is a reliable way to burn calories and stay physically fit. It is important to prepare your body before a run, however, as running can be quite intense of the body. An improper warmup or worse, none at all, can lead to injury and overall pain down the road. Below are 5 basic stretches and other warmup drills to do before running to prevent injury and improve your performance.

  1. Hip Flexor Stretch
  2. Glute Raise and Hold
  3. Hamstring Stretch
  4. Groin Stretch
  5. Calf Bounces

Remember to not overdo your stretching and drills if you're feeling worn and hurt. Some you may need to skip, or maybe you will add new things to your routine. Also, this is a simple quick warmup for slower converstaional paced runs. Intense runs such as thresholds or V02 Max will require an extended warmup routine for proper performance.
